Our Mission

Beyond Bias with Dr. Kebe is a platform dedicated to honest, rigorous, and compassionate examination of systemic bias within the contemporary healthcare system. Through evidence-informed analysis and lived experience, we work to advance equity, accountability, and humane care for all.

Why This Work Matters

Healthcare disparities are not accidental. They are the result of historical, social, and institutional forces that shape access, treatment, and outcomes. Systemic bias in healthcare contributes to:

  • Delayed or missed diagnoses
  • Inequitable treatment and pain management
  • Erosion of patient trust
  • Disproportionate morbidity and mortality

Addressing these inequities requires transparency, education, and sustained community engagement.

What We Do

  • Publish accessible, evidence-based commentary on healthcare inequities
  • Center patient narratives and community experiences
  • Facilitate dialogue among clinicians, scholars, and advocates
  • Promote structural accountability and policy-informed change
